About Dj Vistar

Dj Vistar is an Atlanta based Kenyan Dj. Born and raised in East Africa, he uses his diverse and unique taste of music to entertain crowds from different backgrounds. Dj Vistar has blessed different stages playing different genres of music including Afrobeat, Reggae, Dancehall, Hip-Hop, Rnb, Trap and house music just to name a few.

Discography

Dj Vistar first started playing music in high school where he entertained his classmates during social functions. His love for music grew when he relocated to Atlanta, Ga where he worked as a full-time nightclub Dj while going to school as a fulltime college student.

Dj Vistar has had a chance to work alongside well-known and up-and -coming artists including superstars like Akon, Wale, Mavado, Demarco just to name a few. Dj Vistar has contributed in curating and producing some popular concerts and shows such as 'The PXP Festival', 'Reggae in the Park', 'Rum and Bass' and 'The Greek Fest'.
